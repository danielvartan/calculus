2.2 Complex numbers

Reference: Stewart, Redlin, and Watson (2016)

For the complex number \(\mathcal{z} = a + bi\)

  • The conjugate is \(\overline{\mathcal{z}} = a - bi\)
  • The modulus is \(|\mathcal{z}| = \sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}\)
  • The argument is \(\theta\), where \(\tan \theta = b / a\)

Stewart, James, Lothar Redlin, and Saleem Watson. 2016. Precalculus: Mathematics for Calculus. 7th ed. Cengage Learning.
